Katsu Curry is a delicious Japanese dish consisting of breaded and fried chicken cutlets served with a rich curry sauce and rice.

カツカレー

How to cook Katsu Curry with ingredients

  • Curry Sauce Mix
  • Cooked Rice
  • Onion sliced & diced
  • Potato sliced & diced
  • Carrot sliced & diced
  • Chicken Cutlet
  • Fukujinzuke

Steps on how to cook Katsu Curry step by step

  • Step 1: Put oil into a pot and sauté onion pieces until the edges start browning
  • Step 2: Add potato and carrot pieces to the pot and stir
  • Step 3: Add water and bring it to a boil. Remove scum as it rises
  • Step 4: Add curry roux and cook
  • Step 5: Cook chicken cutlets and slice them
  • Step 6: Put cooked rice on one side of a serving plate, place cutlet pieces next to the rice
  • Step 7: Pour curry on the side next to the cutlet away from the rice
  • Step 8: Place a small amount of fukujinzuke on the plate
  • Step 9: Put them all together, and enjoy
